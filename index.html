Welcome to Sabah Home Care

Locally owned care, rooted in Northern Virginia

Sabah Home Care is a locally owned, Virginia-licensed home care agency based in Chantilly — caring for families throughout Northern Virginia.

Founded in 2020 and led by our president, Zohra Hassanzadeh, we were built on a simple belief: every person deserves to age with dignity, comfort, and independence — in the home they love.

The word Sabah means "morning" — a fresh start, a new day. That's the spirit we bring to every visit: a reassuring presence at the start of each day, and steady, compassionate support through every hour after. Every care plan begins with an in-person conversation, so the caregiver we match feels less like a service and more like family.

Our Home Care Services

Personalized care for every stage of life

Whether your loved one needs a few hours of companionship a week or skilled, around-the-clock support, we tailor every plan to fit their needs and budget.

Personal Care

Respectful, hands-on help with bathing, dressing, grooming, mobility, toileting, and the daily activities that keep life comfortable.

Companion Care

Friendly company, conversation, errands, transportation, meal prep, and light housekeeping to brighten every day at home.

Skilled Nursing

Physician-supervised nursing care — medication management, wound care, monitoring, and clinical support delivered safely at home.

Therapy Services

Physical, occupational, and speech-language therapy to restore strength, mobility, and confidence after illness or injury.

Respite & 24-Hour Care

Short-term relief for family caregivers and continuous, round-the-clock or live-in support so your loved one is never alone.

Alzheimer's & Dementia Care

Specially trained caregivers use routine, patience, and gentle redirection to preserve dignity and bring calm to each day.
